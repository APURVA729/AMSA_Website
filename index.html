Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join this organization??

Kindly Please contact our Membership & Development Team. They will help you with their fullest of abilities. For more details please check the membership section (access to membership tab) of our website.

What are the benefits of joining this organization?

AMSA-KG is affiliated to the IFMSA & AMSA-International which are the world's leading medical students organisations. Hence by joining AMSA-KG you can be assured that you will get plenty of chances to gain knowledge, and clinical exposure through our local health care projects. Apart from this you can get golden opportunities to present yourself on an international platform in terms of professional and research exchange programs.

How can this organization help us as future health care professionals?

AMSA-KG envisions to develop a culture of academically competent , global oriented , culturally sensitive students of medicine who can serve as role models in the health profession. Hence through our events and programs you can improve your medical and clinical knowledge, gain experiences and even develop your professional as well as leadership skills.

With whom we are affiliated?

AMSA-Kyrgyzstan is affiliated to International Federation Medical Students Association ( IFMSA ) and Asian Medical Students Association-International ( AMSA-International ). Currently AMSA-Kyrgyzstan have Candidate Member Status in IFMSA and Observer Member Status in AMSA-International.

What are the activities done under this organization?

The activities we do are according to our focus areas which ranges from Medical Education, Public Health, Human Rights and Peace, Reproductive health and Rights Including HIV and AIDS. We also aim to work on Student Exchange Programs. Our events can help you to become competent in terms of academics. For More details please visit our activities section of the website (Here give access to our ACTIVITIES section).

I want to get involved in various Events, how can I Participate in the events?

We are updating or promoting our activities & events through our Social Media handles and our website. Kindly please follow our Social Media handles for more information and also you can contact our respective Program Coordinators and Membership and Development team.

Who are our Partners?

AMSA-KG has its partnership with ISM Edu. Tech. Pvt. Ltd. and ISM Edutech Pvt. Ltd. is considered as one of the leading education consultants in India having its head office in Gurgaon and 50+ Associate/Agent offices all over India. We also have Agent offices across the globe namely GEORGIA, KYRGYZSTAN, KAZAKHSTAN & TAJIKISTAN.

Which are our Local Member Organisations (LMO)?

1. International Higher School of Medicine , Bishkek.
2. Kyrgyz State Medical Academy , Bishkek.
3. Ala Too International University-Medical Faculty , Bishkek.
4. ISM-IUK Eastern Medical Campus , Bishkek.
5. International Higher School of Medicine , Cholpon Ata (Issyk Kul Campus).

What are our conditions regarding the membership fees ?

At present AMSA-Kyrgyzstan is not collecting annual membership fees from individual members. We are collecting annual membership fees from our full member LMOs ( Student Representative Body ) or from the respective university on behalf of their LMO or students. If you want more clarification regarding membership fees then do not hesitate to contact our Membership and Development team.
