SCORA

What is SCORA ? A standing committee on Sexual and Reproductive Health Including HIV and AIDS is a group of passionate individuals who are committed to promote sexual and reproductive health and rights and are dedicated to creating positive change in our local community.

SCORA HISTORY: In 1992 ,a new working group in IFMSA was formed, named SCOAS(Standing Committee on AIDS and Sexually Transmitted Diseases ). The activities in SCOAS later developed from HIV/STIs advocacy and awareness campaigns, to cover a wide range of reproductive health related issues. This led to a change of focus of the committee in 1998,resulting in a new name : the Standing committee on Reproductive Health including AIDS or SCORA. With a large number of members - SCORAngels as they are called - SCORA has grown wide in its work, centered around the 5 focus areas which are strongly related to sexual and reproductive health and rights. In 2004 the name of SCORA changed once more to the Standing Committee on Sexual and Reproductive Health including HIV/AIDS as it is more adequate in terms of topics and problems that SCORA is targeting its action.

SCORA focused areas :
1.Comprehensive Sexuality Education.
2. Maternal Health and access to safe abortion
3. Sexuality and gender identity.
4. Gender-Based Violence.
5. HIV and other STIs.

About SCORA-KYRGYZSTAN(SCORA-KG)

SCORA- KG encompasses all the focal areas of SCORA. Some major issues of our region are Gender based violence,unsafe abortion,sex education, gender discrimination and high stigma on different sexuality and gender identity along with the STIs and AIDs at high level. SCORAngels in Kyrgyzstan are dealing with the above issues which are not addressed due to conservative mindset and fear of taboo in our society.SCORA-KG pursues its aims without any political,religious,social,racial,sexual or any other discrimination.

OUR VISION : To create a world in which every individual is empowered to exercise their sexual and reproductive health and rights.A society where everyone is free to speak and express their views on Sexual and Reproductive Health.

OUR MISSION: To provide our members with tools necessary to advocate for sexual and reproductive health rights within the respective communities in culturally sensitive fashion.Our main mission is to create a positive change at the local, regional, and international level with the help of SCORAngels.

OUR OBJECTIVES:

  • To raise awareness on topics related to HIV and AIDS and sexual and reproductive health.
  • To decrease the stigma and discrimination against people living with HIV and AIDS.
  • To raise awareness and increase knowledge about facts, scientific research, global agreements and documents concerning sexual and reproductive health.
  • Advocating for policies concerning sexual and reproductive health and represent medical student worldwide.
  • Collaborate and facilitate joint actions concerning medical education, public health, and human rights.
  • Promote positive sexuality and healthy sexual life.
  • Provide tools for capacity building for future healthcare professionals in terms of sexual and reproductive health and rights
